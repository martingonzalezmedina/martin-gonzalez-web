The Story Behind the Song

Norma My Dear

"Norma My Dear" is a heartfelt love letter written in the language of music. It is dedicated to the woman who has been a source of peace, strength and unconditional love through every season of life.

Whenever sadness appears, her embrace brings comfort. Whenever tears are close, her smile has the power to bring hope again. She is the person who fills every empty space with kindness, patience and genuine love.

This song was born from gratitude. It is a tribute to a woman whose value cannot be measured, someone truly irreplaceable. More than a romantic partner, she is a best friend, a companion and the greatest blessing life has offered.

Written in English with a soft listening romantic style, this song expresses the deepest appreciation for a love that inspires, heals and gives meaning to every new day.

"Norma My Dear" is more than a song. It is a promise of love and a heartfelt thank you to the woman who changed everything simply by being herself.

Song Quote

"You are not only the love of my life... you are the peace my heart was always searching for."
